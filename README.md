# Panopto Video Downloader

A simple app to download and watch Panopto videos offline.

## What it does

- Enter a Panopto video URL and download it for offline viewing
- Works with both single videos and dual videos (webcam + screen share)
- Saves your downloaded videos in a library so you can watch them anytime
- No internet needed after downloading

## How to use

1. Paste a Panopto video URL
2. Click Download and wait for it to finish
3. Watch your video!
4. Find all your downloaded videos in the Archive section

## Features

- ✅ Download videos from Panopto URLs
- ✅ Watch videos offline
- ✅ Dual video support (webcam and screen share side by side)
- ✅ Video library to manage your downloads
- ✅ Playback controls (speed, size, fullscreen)
